Frequently Asked Questions

      Here are some of the most common questions we receive

Question 1: What are your two membership options: Done-With-You and Done-For-You?

We offer businesses two options in building automated outbound systems that generate qualified appointments.

In our Done With You program, we guide you step-by-step to build your own in-house outbound system. By the end of the program, your team will have the knowledge, processes, and systems in place to run it independently.

In our Done For You program, our team builds, sets up, and manages the outbound system for you, so you can focus on closing deals and growing your business.

Whichever program you choose, by the end you will have a fully automated or delegated outbound system that runs hands-free and consistently generates new clients without requiring your daily involvement.

Question 2: How long does it take to get set up?

Most clients are fully set up within 5 DAYS.

During this time we configure your outreach infrastructure, set up the necessary tools, and prepare your initial lead lists so your system is ready to start generating conversations and booked appointments.

Question 3: How is this different from other lead generation agencies or programs?

Most lead generation agencies simply sell you leads or run campaigns for you — and when you stop paying, the leads stop too.

At Agency Advanta, we help you build the system inside your agency, giving you the frameworks and infrastructure to generate consistent qualified appointments whenever you need them.

In other words, you’re not just buying leads — you’re building your own growth engine.

Question 4: Are you spamming people?

No. Our outreach approach focuses on targeted and relevant conversations with prospects who are a strong fit for your service.

Instead of sending mass generic messages, we teach structured outreach strategies that prioritize personalization, value-driven messaging, and responsible prospecting practices.

The goal is to start meaningful business conversations with qualified prospects, not to send spam.

Question 5: What results can I expect?

Results vary depending on your offer, market, and consistency of implementation. That said, the goal of the Agency Advanta system is to help you generate consistent qualified sales conversations with potential clients through structured outbound outreach.

Clients who implement the systems properly often see improvements in:
    1. Consistent booked appointments
    2. Increased qualified conversations with prospects
    3. More predictable client acquisition pipelines

Like any system, results depend on execution and commitment to the process.
